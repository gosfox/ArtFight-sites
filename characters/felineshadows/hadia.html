Desc

In ancient Egypt, a colony of Egyptians (later called Astro-Egyptians) traveled into space with the help of mysterious "aliens". Their goal was to oversee and protect Earth from the shadows. Eventually, the "aliens" turned away from humans, becoming intrusive and influencing all important events. Hadia, a descendant of ancient Egyptians, is a member of the Neutralization Division, which specializes in eliminating spies and preventing them from harming humanity. Seeing that a small group of humans learned about the "Cays"*, she decided to use their unique skills and recruit them to the ranks of AE.
